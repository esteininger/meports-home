Built for complex conditions

Starting with conditions that benefit most from personalized, data-driven approaches

MCAS

Mast Cell Activation Syndrome

Example Use Case:
Track how different foods, supplements, or environmental triggers affect your heart rate variability, sleep quality, and recovery metrics. Test elimination diets, antihistamines, or stress management techniques to identify what actually helps reduce your symptom flares.

POTS

Postural Orthostatic Tachycardia Syndrome

Example Use Case:
Monitor how hydration protocols, compression garments, or specific exercises impact your heart rate patterns and energy levels throughout the day. Measure the effectiveness of different salt intake levels or pacing strategies on your overall function.

EDS

Ehlers-Danlos Syndrome

Example Use Case:
Track how different physical therapy approaches, joint support strategies, or pain management techniques affect your sleep quality, stress levels, and daily function. Test the impact of specific exercises, braces, or lifestyle modifications on your overall well-being.
